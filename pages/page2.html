MATCH UP



  • Start the game by flipping a card.

  • Then try to find another card that has the same image as the
          first. If you can't find a pair, the flipped cards will be flipped       back with the face.

  • Try to remember these images as it becomes easier to find pairs       the longer you play.

  • There will be a timer of 60 seconds in which you have to find the       pairs.

  • When you find all the pairs you will win the game.
